Story Summary

Chapter One

Harry and Michael race to Cook County Hospital to stop a dangerous ghost. Along the way, Michael asks Harry if he loves Susan Rodriguez and encourages him to consider marriage.

  • Harry Dresden: A professional wizard in Chicago who uses a staff, blasting rod, and a silver pentacle amulet to investigate supernatural disturbances.
  • Michael Carpenter: A Knight of the Cross and a carpenter by trade who wields the holy sword Amoracchius; he first met Harry years prior when he rescued his wife from a dragon.

Chapter Two

They find the hospital nursery under an enchantment cast by the ghost of Agatha Hagglethorn. Harry attempts to banish her using “ghost dust,” but the spirit attacks him before fleeing into the Nevernever.

  • Agatha Hagglethorn: A nineteenth-century high-society ghost who accidentally smothered her own child and now seeks to “protect” infants by drawing them into eternal sleep.

Chapter Three

In a flashback, Harry is at his office when Susan Rodriguez calls requesting an interview about recent ghost activity. Later, a young woman named Lydia arrives seeking protection.

  • Susan Rodriguez: A reporter for the Arcane tabloid and Harry’s love interest.
  • Lydia: A magically gifted young woman seeking a talisman to protect her from a hostile spirit.

Chapter Four

Lydia reveals she suffers from Cassandra’s Tears, prophetic visions no one believes. Harry gives her a protective talisman and sends her to Father Forthill for sanctuary.

  • Father Forthill: A priest at Saint Mary of the Angels who provides spiritual and physical protection.

Chapter Five

Harry and Michael enter Agatha’s demesne in the Nevernever, resembling nineteenth-century Chicago. Harry notices barbed wire embedded in the ghost’s spirit, suggesting deliberate torture.

Chapter Six

Leanansidhe, Harry’s faerie godmother, appears with hellhounds to claim him under an old bargain. Harry escapes by dumping iron-filled ghost dust on her.

  • Leanansidhe (Lea): A powerful sidhe and Harry’s faerie godmother who holds a long-standing claim over him.

Chapter Seven

Harry and Michael are arrested at the hospital but are bailed out by Charity Carpenter. Susan meets Harry afterward, and vampires confront them at his apartment.

  • Charity Carpenter: Michael’s pregnant wife who resents the danger Harry brings into their lives.

Chapter Eight

Kyle and Kelly Hamilton deliver an invitation from Bianca to a masquerade ball. Harry drives them away, and he and Susan spend the night together.

  • Kyle and Kelly Hamilton: Red Court vampire twins serving as Bianca’s heralds.

Chapter Nine

Harry experiences disturbing nightmares. Michael reports that a creature attacked the church where Lydia was hiding, though the consecrated ground held it at bay. Lydia has vanished.

Chapter Ten

Harry visits Mortimer Lindquist, who explains that the barrier between worlds is thinning. He names the new threat “the Nightmare.”

  • Mortimer Lindquist: An ectomancer who can communicate with spirits.

Chapter Eleven

Detectives Rudolph and Stallings take Harry to the home of Micky Malone, where several dead animals are found in the yard.

  • Detective Rudolph: A clean-cut detective with Special Investigations.
  • Detective Sergeant John Stallings: Rudolph’s perceptive and more thoughtful partner.

Chapter Twelve

Harry meets Karrin Murphy inside the Malone home. They find Micky Malone cuffed to his bed in a state of violent madness.

  • Karrin Murphy: Head of Special Investigations at the Chicago P.D.
  • Micky Malone: A former S.I. officer injured in a previous supernatural case.
  • Sonia Malone: Micky’s wife, known for her quiet strength and reliability.

Chapter Thirteen

Using his Sight, Harry sees spiritual barbed wire embedded in Micky’s soul. He removes it, and after a violent struggle, destroys the spell with fire.

Chapter Fourteen

Harry returns to his lab and consults with Bob about the growing supernatural threat.

  • Bob: A spirit of intellect living in a human skull, serving as Harry’s magical assistant.

Chapter Fifteen

Harry and Bob determine that the Nightmare is deliberately agitating spirits. Harry sends Bob out, possessing his cat Mister, to locate Lydia.

Chapter Sixteen

Harry tracks his talisman to an abandoned factory and finds Lydia drugged in a van. Kyle and Kelly attack, but sunlight burns Kelly as the building collapses. The vampires escape with Lydia.

Chapter Seventeen

Drugged by vampire saliva, Harry falls into a dream of a failed raid on the sorcerer Leonid Kravos. The Nightmare enters the dream and devours part of Harry’s magic.

  • Leonid Kravos: A ritual killer and sorcerer previously defeated by Harry and Michael.

Chapter Eighteen

Bob explains that the Nightmare is likely the ghost of Kravos’s demon, seeking revenge. Harry realizes Murphy is the next target.

Chapter Nineteen

Harry finds the Nightmare torturing Murphy at the police station, appearing exactly like him. Harry puts Murphy into a magically protected sleep.

Chapter Twenty

Harry and Michael discover that Charity has been kidnapped and taken to Graceland Cemetery.

Chapter Twenty-One

At the cemetery, Lea offers Harry power in exchange for service. Harry destroys the Nightmare by dragging it into running water. Lea steals Michael’s sword after he drops it during the fight.

Chapter Twenty-Two

Harry recovers in the hospital and realizes the Nightmare was only a pawn. He decides they must attend Bianca’s party to uncover the true culprit.

Chapter Twenty-Three

Harry uses Kravos’s journal to summon and bind the Nightmare so it can only attack him, protecting his friends.

Chapter Twenty-Four

Harry and Michael arrive at Bianca’s masquerade and meet Thomas and Justine.

  • Thomas: A White Court vampire of House Raith who assists Harry.
  • Justine: Thomas’s human companion who begs Harry for help.

Chapter Twenty-Five

Bianca greets Harry with public hostility. Kelly Hamilton is burned when she touches Michael’s faith-protected arm.

  • Bianca St. Claire: A powerful Red Court vampire and Harry’s long-time enemy.

Chapter Twenty-Six

Thomas warns Harry the wine is poisoned. Harry meets Ferrovax, a dragon in human form. Susan arrives uninvited, violating the laws of hospitality.

  • Ferrovax: An ancient dragon appearing as a human noble.

Chapter Twenty-Seven

Lea tricks Susan into trading a year of her memories to save Harry. Susan forgets who Harry is.

Chapter Twenty-Eight

Mavra blocks the group’s escape. Harry realizes she has been manipulating the ghosts.

  • Mavra: A Black Court vampire of immense magical power.

Chapter Twenty-Nine

Bianca holds Court and gives cruel “gifts.” Mavra is given Lydia to sacrifice, and Michael’s sword to destroy. Harry chooses to fight.

Chapter Thirty

A massive battle erupts. Thomas betrays Harry to save Justine. Harry unleashes Pyrofuego, destroying Bianca’s estate. Harry and Michael escape with Lydia, but Susan is left behind with the vampires.
